Privacy Policy

AppShare – APK Backup & Share

Effective date: 2026-05-07  ·  Developer: Daoud  ·  Contact: coolapphelpdesk@gmail.com  ·  Package: coolapp.appshare

1. Overview

AppShare is a local APK management utility. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data is and is not collected when you use AppShare.

AppShare itself collects no personal data. All files and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server operated by the developer.

2. Data Collected by AppShare

All of the following are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted:

Data Where it is stored Transmitted?
List of installed apps Device memory (runtime only) Never
Extracted APK files Device internal storage / Downloads folder Never
APK Vault contents Device internal storage Never
Share history Device SharedPreferences Never
App settings (theme, language) Device SharedPreferences Never

3. Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

AppShare displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA). To serve relevant ads, AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address (used to derive approximate location)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks)
  • Diagnostic data

This data is processed by Google under their own privacy policy. AppShare does not receive, store, or have access to this data.

  • Google's privacy policy
  • How Google uses data from partner apps
  • Opt out: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

GDPR / EEA & UK Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, AdMob will request your consent before collecting data for personalised advertising. You may withdraw consent at any time through your device ad settings.

4. Permissions Used and Why

Permission Reason
QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES Required to list all installed apps so you can browse, extract, and share them. This is the core function of AppShare.
REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES Required to reinstall APK files from your local backup.
INTERNET Required by Google AdMob to serve banner advertisements.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Required by Google AdMob to check connectivity before loading ads.
INSTALL_SHORTCUT Allows AppShare to be added to the device home screen.

No permission is used for any purpose other than what is stated above.

5. Data Sharing

AppShare does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties, except as described in Section 3 (Google AdMob), which operates independently and under its own terms.

6. Data Retention

Since AppShare stores all data locally on your device:

  • APK files and vault: Remain on your device until you delete them from within the app or manually from your file manager.
  • Share history: Remains on your device until you clear it using the "Clear history" button in the app.
  • Settings: Remain on your device until you uninstall the app.

Uninstalling AppShare removes all app settings and history. APK files saved to your Downloads folder are not removed on uninstall.

7. Children's Privacy

AppShare is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not contain content inappropriate for children, but it is a utility app intended for general audiences.

8. Security

All data managed by AppShare is stored locally on your device and protected by Android's built-in application sandboxing. APK files in the app's internal storage are not accessible to other apps. Files saved to the Downloads folder follow standard Android file access rules.
